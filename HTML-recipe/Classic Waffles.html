Classic Waffles

Golden Brown waffles with Syrup and Fresh berries

In a bowl,mix together flour,salt,baking powder and sugar;set aside. Pre-heat waffle iron to desired temperature. In a separate bowl,beat the eggs. Stir in the milk,butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture;beat until blended. Laddle the batter into a pre-heated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp.Serve immediately.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Baking Powder
  • White Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Vanilla Extract

    • Steps

    1. 1. In a large bowl, mix flour,salt,baking powder and sugar;set aside.Preheat the waffle iron to desired temperature.
    2. 2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
    3. 3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crsip. serve immediately.
